Profile

Max Evans is a game designer and poet. His personal work focuses on multi-media art such as AR music making, interactive twine poetry, and experimental educational games. Max focuses on issues related to trans rights, accessibility, and human-machine interactions. He holds a BS from MIT in Mechanical Engineering with a focus in Robotic Design, and a minor in Creative Writing.
